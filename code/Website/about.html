What is Brexit Analysis?

Brexit Analysis is our chosen project as part of our work for CA326.

The system comprised of tweets downloaded from Twitter via Twarc (a Python library designed for the mining of tweets in a JSON format), a back-end MySQL database and a rudimentary sentiment analysis algorithm.

Graphs are provided courtesy of Google Charts

Why Brexit?

The ulitmate goal of our project is to showcase or highlight the relationship between the content of a particular tweet and the location from which it was posted.

As such, we decided upon the control topic of the 2016 Brexit Referendum. The result in which the United Kingdom opted to leave the EU after 43 years of membership is the first of its kind. We felt that this would more than a suitable topic in which we could showcase the differences in opinions from various places around the globe and within the U.K. itself.
